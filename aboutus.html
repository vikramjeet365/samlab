About SAM Lab

SAM Lab is a leading research laboratory at the Centre for Ocean, River, Atmosphere, and Land Sciences (CORAL), IIT Kharagpur. Led by Dr. Mukunda Dev Behera, the lab specializes in geospatial analysis and ecological modelling. Our research integrates cutting-edge tools and methodologies to analyze and understand various environmental and ecological phenomena.

Research Highlights

Species Distribution Model / Ecological Niche Model
Prediction of species distribution under changing environmental and climate conditions.
Species Community Analysis
Assessment of functional diversity and ecological interactions within ecosystems.
Landscape Ecological Models
Analysis of habitat fragmentation, connectivity, and landscape-scale ecological processes.
Geo-statistics & Causality
Understanding of spatial patterns, relationships, and causality in environmental variables.
GIS & Image Processing
Land cover classification, change detection, spatial pattern analysis, and remote sensing applications.
Soil and Water Modelling
Hydrological modeling, water flow simulation, and sediment transport assessment.
Vegetation Carbon Dynamics
Estimation of carbon fluxes, biomass dynamics, and ecosystem productivity changes.
Forest Fire Modelling
Simulation of fire spread patterns and wildfire risk assessment.
LiDAR & TLS Analysis
High-resolution forest structure mapping, canopy characterization, and biomass estimation.
AI / ML / DL Applications
Feature extraction, Above Ground Biomass (AGB) estimation, and causality analysis.
Climate Modelling
Projection of climate change impacts and variability using CMIP6 datasets.
Ecosystem Services Evaluation
Quantification of ecosystem services for conservation planning and sustainable management.
Radiative Transfer Models (RTM)
Analysis of vegetation reflectance, energy balance, and remote sensing data.
Carbon Accounting
Measurement and tracking of carbon stocks, sequestration, and emissions for climate mitigation.
